taking the shot

Meter the scene.

Compose your image, focus on your subject, and let the camera meter the scene withouth the ND filter. Take note of the shutter speed suggested by the camera. 

 

Set the ND filter level.

Set the filter notation (found in settings) that is used on the ND filter. Choose the filter density/f-stop reduction that you want to use. 

 

Set the base shutter speed.

Dial in the shutter speed suggested by the camera when it metered the scene. Press calculate to get the adjusted shutter speed.

 

Start the countdown.

 Start the timer and close the shutter when the time runs out.
